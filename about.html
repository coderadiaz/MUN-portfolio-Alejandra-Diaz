Hi I am Alejandra Diaz

MUN counselor based in Venezuela

I was a member of San Ignacio School HMUN 2023 delegation.
In the Harvard Model of United Nations conference of 2023, my assigned committe was a 178-delegate General Assembly. During my preparation process, the question "What will make ME stand out from so many people?" popped up in my mind numerous times. Many delegates will make good speeches, bring up great solutions and have distinguashed leadership.
That is how I came up with a strategy that would fit my committee, associating myself and narrative to flowers. When the conference time arrives, I followed my plan by heart. As a result I won Best Delegate in the World Conference of Women Committee.
Nowadays, I guide delegates to go beyond the basics. Delegates who are already confident on their public speaking and negotiation but want to uplevel to a strategy that makes them stand out from the rest, making themselves and what they represent a strong brand that fits with the committe.
