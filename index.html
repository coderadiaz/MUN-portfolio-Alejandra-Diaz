Why HMUN?

HMUN is one of the greatest opportunities that you will encounter as a student to practice soft skills that you will use for a the rest of your life. It will help you master public speaking, people management, research and negotiation. Besides, you will be able to build connections with competent children around the world.

Why should you pursue an award?

An award in HMUN can bring you many academic and professional opportunities. It is a reflect of your leadership, negotiation, research, and critical thinking; which are appealing traits for college admissions and recruiting companies.

How to be outstanding?

Every MUNer knows that good speeches, getting into Q&A and making good points and motions are important factors to do well in committee. However, to significantly increase your chances of getting an award, you need to go beyond the basics by practicing design-thinking in your preparation process.
